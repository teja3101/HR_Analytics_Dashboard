2. Description
This HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of workforce dynamics with a strong focus on employee attrition and demographic insights. It highlights key HR metrics such as total employee count, attrition count, attrition rate, active employees, and average age.
The dashboard enables analysis of attrition across departments, gender, education fields, job roles, and age groups. It also visualizes employee distribution by age and job satisfaction ratings, helping HR teams understand workforce composition and engagement levels. Overall, this dashboard supports strategic HR decision-making by identifying attrition drivers and workforce trends.
